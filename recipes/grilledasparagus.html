Grilled Asparagus

When asparagus season hits, take advantage while you can! Make Asparagus Soup, Asparagus Tart, and of course, fire up the grill to cook as much grilled asparagus as possible.

Ingredients

  • Asparagus
  • Olive Oil
  • Seasonings of Choice

    • Steps

    1. Trim the Asparagus

      2. The woody end is too hard and fibrous to eat.

    2. Oil and Season

      3. Be sure the asparagus has a nice coating so the tips don’t burn.

    3. Lay on the Grill

      4. Place perpendicular to the grates to get nice char marks.

    4. Grill.

      5. se medium-high heat, about 375°F to 400°F. Grill for 6 to 10 minutes over until fork tender.

    5. Serve

      6. Grilled asparagus tastes great both warm and at room temperature.
